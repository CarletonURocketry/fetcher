int16_t *  x,
int16_t *  y,
int16_t *  z 
)

Converts acceleration in milli-Gs per LSB to meters per second squared. Results are stored back in the pointers themselves. Passing NULL as any of the pointers will result in the calculation being skipped.

Parameters
acc_fsrThe full scale range of the accelerometer.
xA pointer to the X component of the acceleration in milli-Gs per LSB.
yA pointer to the Y component of the acceleration in milli-Gs per LSB.
zA pointer to the Z component of the acceleration in milli-Gs per LSB.
